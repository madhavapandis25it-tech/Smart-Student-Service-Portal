Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

To submit an Original Certificate request, navigate to My Applications from the sidebar, or click Applications → Original Certificate. Fill in the required details including certificate type and purpose, upload the necessary documents, and click Submit Application. Your application will then enter the approval workflow.
The approval process involves multiple stages: Tutor → HOD → Office Staff → DRS. Each stage is reviewed sequentially. The total time depends on the responsiveness of each approver. Typically, straightforward applications are processed within 5–7 working days.
Once an application is submitted and enters the approval workflow, modifications are not possible until the application has been reviewed. If you need to make changes, you may cancel the application (if it is still at the Tutor stage) and submit a new one. For applications already under review, please contact the respective approver.
For urgent requests, please contact the Office Staff directly during working hours. You can also reach the college administration at admin@psnacet.edu.in with the subject line marked as "Urgent – Certificate Application" along with your application ID.
You can change your password by navigating to Settings from the sidebar. Enter your current password, set a new password (minimum 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, and a number), and confirm it. If you have forgotten your current password, please contact the administration office to request a password reset.
